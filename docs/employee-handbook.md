# Employee Handbook

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9am–6pm Monday to Friday. Flexible arrangements must be approved by your manager.

## Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 20 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Public holidays: as per regional calendar

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act with integrity, respect colleagues, and protect company data.

## IT Policy
- Do not share passwords or credentials
- Use company-issued devices for work
- Report security incidents to security@company.internal immediately

## Benefits
- Health insurance
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Annual learning budget: $1,500
