# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed 90 days of employment are eligible for remote work.

## Requirements
- Stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Must be available during core hours: 10am–3pm in your team's primary timezone

## Equipment
The company provides a laptop. Additional peripherals may be requested via the IT portal with manager approval.

## Security
- Always use the company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi without VPN
- Lock your screen when stepping away
